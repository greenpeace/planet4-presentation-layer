Join the people power movement

Modest goals wont walk us back from the edge of climate catastrophe. We need to dive into uncharted territories. Business as usual will only keep the planet on its current path. We need to collaborate with millions of new change makers. We need to your collaboration. Because Greenpeace only works with you.

Explore a positive future vision

Think of the possibilities. A planet of peace and health. Where forests spread with ease, growing each day. Where oceans are full of life. Where our food is delicious, nutritious, and grown with love. Where taking a deep breath means inhaling sweet-smelling air. Where everyone has the safety, dignity and beauty we all deserve.

Values

Greenpeace uses non-violent, creative confrontation to expose global environmental problems, and to force the solutions which are essential to a green and peaceful future.

See what we stand for
Histroy

"A trip for life, and for peace": thats how Irving Stowe, one of the co-founders of Greenpeace, described the plan to sail a boat to the Arctic Ocean to stop the testing of a nuclear bomb. Irving didnt know it then, but the trip would last for decades and change the world.
