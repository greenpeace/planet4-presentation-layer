The tale of the Naic Whale

A massive whale is unveiled on the shores of Sea Side Beach resort in Naic, Cavite. The 15m x 3m blue whale, which looked to be in the late stages of decomposition, immediately captured the attention of nearby communities and beachgoers. As groups gatheredto haul off the dead whale, they noticed that its entire body was made of plastic wastes found in the ocean.
