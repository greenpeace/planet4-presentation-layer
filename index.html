Why this matters

The climate needs us. Livestock, especially beef, accounts for 14% of greenhouse gas emissions (CO2, methane, nitrous oxide) worldwide, which exceeds the greenhouse gases of all transportation vehicles driving on the road worldwide. Help us change the broken system.
